Shrutz Healing

Alternative Psychotherapy / Trauma Recovery / Shadow work

I am a Certified Practitioner of Teal Swan's Completion Process. It is a powerful process that heals trauma, unhealthy patterns, PTSD and more.

We will be:

  • Working on healing your mental, emotional & spiritual self
  • Uncovering the roots of your diseases and ailments
  • Discovering and unlocking patterns that are perplexing you
  • Gaining new insights and knowledge about who you are

The pain you are experiencing today is rooted in your childhood. We will be doing shadow work / inner child work to discover what you went through and what you felt, and perhaps more importantly, what you didn't feel, and are yearning to feel. I am here to connect with you, hear your stories, share your pain and facilitate your inner healing work. I invite you to hold my hand as we both dive in and discover the hidden vulnerable parts of you that are begging to be seen, heard and felt.

Specialty

Some of the traumas I specialize in are Physical abuse and/or Torture, Emotional abuse and/or Gaslighting, Domestic violence, Complex PTSD, Surviving Cancer - surgery / chemotherapy / radiation and after effects.

Feelings of isolation, being unwanted and unloved, rage and anger, extreme shame, self hate, powerlessness, hopelessness and being suicidal.

We are here to experience the opposite of the pain we went through as children instead of constantly re-living it. The Completion Process transforms that pain so that it ceases to reflect in our lives. It enables us to experience togetherness instead of isolation, love and safety instead of rejection and abuse, equality instead of hierarchy, respectful boundaries instead of violence, etc.

Sessions

I live and work in San Francisco, CA and offer both face to face and online sessions.

Face to face sessions can be done at my home or another safe place we both agree on. Online sessions are via Google Hangouts, Skype, FaceTime or Zoom. My rate is $80/session, and I offer packages of 3, 5 & 10 sessions at a discount. I accept PayPal after the session is over.

I work Monday through Friday 8:30am to 4pm PT (Pacific Time)

A typical Completion Process session lasts from 1 to 2 hours.
